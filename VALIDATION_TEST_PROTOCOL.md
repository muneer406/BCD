# BCD Validation Test — Subject Protocol

Thank you for helping validate the BCD (Breast Changes Detection) app!
This test will take about **1.5 hours** and involves **3 capture sessions**.

## What We're Testing

We need to prove the app can tell the difference between:
- **Normal day-to-day variation** (should show LOW change scores)
- **Real physical changes** (should show HIGHER change scores)

You'll do 3 sessions: two normal ones to measure consistency, and one with a small intentional change to test sensitivity.

---

## Before You Start

**Preparation (5 min):**
- Choose a room with **consistent lighting** (natural daylight is best, avoid direct sun)
- Wear the **same clothing/top** for all 3 sessions
- Remove jewelry that might shift between captures
- Clear the capture area — stand in front of a plain wall if possible
- Read ALL instructions below before starting anything
- Have a **small sticker** (5-10mm, any color), a **washable marker**, or a piece of **tape** ready for Session C

**Important:** Do not change clothes, lighting, or room between sessions.

---

## Session A — Normal Baseline

**Time:** Start here.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Open the BCD app and log in |
| 2 | Navigate to the Capture page |
| 3 | Capture a **full 6-angle session** (front, left, right, up, down, raised) |
| 4 | Take your time — ensure each angle is well-framed and in focus |
| 5 | Save the session |
| 6 | Write down the current time: **Session A time = _________** |

✅ Session A complete. **Wait 30 minutes** before Session B.

*During the wait:* Stay in the same room if possible. Keep the same clothing on.

---

## Session B — Normal Duplicate (30 min after A)

**Time:** Exactly 30 minutes after Session A.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Do NOT change anything about your appearance or position |
| 2 | Open the BCD app |
| 3 | Capture **another full 6-angle session** — try to stand in the same spot, same distance from camera |
| 4 | Save the session |
| 5 | Write down the current time: **Session B time = _________** |

✅ Session B complete. This is the most critical comparison — Sessions A and B should look nearly identical to the algorithm.

**Wait another 30 minutes** before Session C.

---

## Session C — With Intentional Change (30 min after B)

**Time:** Exactly 30 minutes after Session B.

Before capturing, you'll add a small physical change:

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Apply **ONE** of the following to your **RIGHT** side only (breast area): |
| | • A small sticker (5-10mm round) placed on your skin |
| | • A small dot drawn with washable marker |
| | • A small piece of tape |
| 2 | Take a **reference photo** of the mark with your phone (for your records — not in the app) |
| 3 | Open the BCD app |
| 4 | Capture a **full 6-angle session** as normal |
| 5 | Save the session |
| 6 | Write down the time: **Session C time = _________** |
| 7 | Remove the sticker/mark after finishing |

✅ All 3 sessions complete! You're done. Thank you!
